ABOUT

CopyCase will help you to find full text cases in a single click of a button. Our algorithm will match the cases on the internet and automatically writes it on a word document file that will be packaged in a zip file.
 A solution to eliminate the very time consuming and agonizing process of manual scraping of the internet for full cases and copying each word of it. In just a single click, our algorithm can give you any number of cases it can find.


How to Use:

Just follow the format shown on the search bar, which is your search items separated by commaâ€™s ( , ). Then the site will automatically download a zip file containing your cases in a word document format. You can click the checkboxes to select your site references.
